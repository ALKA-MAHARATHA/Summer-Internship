Variegated Hinoki Bonsai, Golden Hinoki Bonsai is a slow-growing, evergreen tree native to Japan, valued for its fine-textured foliage, elegant form, and aromatic wood.


Plant Care Guide

Water: Water when the soil is 80% - 90% dry.Gently water thoroughly and allow water to drain from the bottom. Do not allow plant to dry up completely for more than a day.

Light:They require as much direct sunlight as possible, placing them in the balcony or near the window would be ideal.

Tips:All plants come with a comprehensive care guide.
